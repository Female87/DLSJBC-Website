About De La Salle John Bosco College

De La Salle John Bosco College is a PAASCU-accredited Lasallian institution anchored in faith, service, and academic excellence.

Our Story

Founded through the guidance of the Lasallian community, the college has grown into a school that nurtures young people through instruction, service, and leadership.

Its programs span basic education, senior high, college, and TVET pathways, ensuring learners are prepared for life and work.

Mission and Vision

The school continues to shape compassionate, innovative, and service-driven Lasallians through a student-centered learning environment.

We remain committed to academic progress, community care, and values formation.
