Admissions

Join the DLSJBC community

From pre-enrollment to final registration, the college provides a clear pathway for students and families to begin their academic journey.

Enrollment steps

  • Coordinate with the guidance office and admissions team.
  • Secure the forms and requirements for the grade or program level.
  • Submit the necessary documents to the registrar and finance office.
  • Complete payment and orientation to finalize enrollment.

Requirements

  • School records, birth certificate, and identification documents.
  • Certificate of good moral character and medical clearance.
  • Additional documents for transfer or college applicants.
