Summer Sports Clinic and Enrichment Classes

Orientation and opening of enrichment classes and sports clinic is a great opportunity to stay active and learn during the summer break.

The Summer Sports Clinic and Enrichment Classes offer students a welcoming place to learn, play, and build confidence during the break. The program combines sporting activities with enrichment opportunities.

These initiatives reflect the school's commitment to broader development beyond academics and encourage active participation from interested learners.
